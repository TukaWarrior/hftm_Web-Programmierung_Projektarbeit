The Doom Slayer is a legend forged in courage and resilience. In a world besieged by Hell's demonic forces, he fought fearlessly until an ill-fated mission dragged him into Hell. There, he endured gruelling trials, emerging as an unstoppable force. Clad in the iconic green Praetor Suit, he seeks vengeance upon Hell's hordes, his origin a tale of transformation from marine to a force of nature.
