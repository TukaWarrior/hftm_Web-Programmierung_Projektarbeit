In the heart of Gotham, young Bruce Wayne, heir to the Wayne fortune, witnessed his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, gunned down by a desperate mugger. This tragedy lit a fire within him, spurring Bruce to embark on a global journey of self-improvement. He trained with the world's best martial artists and detectives, honing his skills and conquering his fears. A chance encounter with a cave filled with bats inspired him to create a symbol of fear and justice. Returning to Gotham, he became the enigmatic Batman, using his wealth, intellect, and combat abilities to protect his city from the darkest threats.
