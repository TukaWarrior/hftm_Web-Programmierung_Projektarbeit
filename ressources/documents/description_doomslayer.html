Doom Slayer, the unstoppable force against Hell's armies, donning the iconic Praetor Suit. He's the universe's last line of defense against the forces of Hell. His enigmatic origin molded him into a near-immortal, unyielding champion. With an insatiable thirst for retribution and a vast arsenal, he's the ultimate nightmare for Hell's denizens. When the world faces demonic doom, Doom Slayer is the name you'll want to invoke.
