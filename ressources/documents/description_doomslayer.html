Doom Slayer, the unstoppable force of demon-slaying mayhem! Clad in his iconic Praetor Suit, he's the universe's last line of defense against the forces of Hell. His origin, shrouded in mystery, transformed an ordinary soldier into a relentless, near-immortal, and utterly unyielding champion. Armed with a vast arsenal and fueled by an insatiable desire for retribution, he's the ultimate nightmare for Hell's denizens. When the world teeters on the brink of demonic doom, Doom Slayer is the name you'll want to invoke.
