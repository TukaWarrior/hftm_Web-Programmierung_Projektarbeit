Lara Croft, the iconic Tomb Raider, began her adventures as an English archaeologist and heiress. Her transformation into a fearless explorer unfolded when she embarked on her first expedition. Trapped on an isolated island, she honed her survival skills, emerging as the tenacious, dual-wielding adventurer we know today. Lara's insatiable curiosity and unyielding spirit drive her to uncover ancient mysteries and seek legendary treasures.
